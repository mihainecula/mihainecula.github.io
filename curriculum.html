Conf. dr. Mihai Necula

Personal

Surname, name: Mihai Necula, born on June 13, 1959 in Vatra-Dornei, Suceava, Romanian citizen, married, two children, residing in Iasi, Al. Sucidava no. 13, place of work: "Al. I. Cuza" University, Faculty of Mathematics.

Studies

Public School No. 10 Galati (1966-1974), "Vasile Alecsandri" National College, Galati (1974-1978), Faculty of Mathematics, "Al. I. Cuza" University of Iasi (1979-1983), department of mathematics. PhD in Mathematics from December 19, 2003, granted by "Al. I. Cuza" University, Thesis: The viability of time-dependent domains for differential inclusions, Scientific leader: Prof. Dr. Ioan I. Vrabie.

Cuza" University (1986-1990), researcher, CP III - Institute of Mathematics of Romanian Academy Iasi (1990 -1990), teaching assistant (1990-1991), assistant professor (1991-2007) and associate professor (2008-present), Faculty of Mathematics, "Al. I. Cuza" University.
